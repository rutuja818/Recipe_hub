Chocolate Hazelnut

Ingredients: - 1 cup hazelnuts - 1 cup dark chocolate chips - 1/2 cup powdered sugar - 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder - 1/4 teaspoon salt - 2 tablespoons unsalted butter

Instructions:

  • Roast hazelnuts at 350째F (180째C) for 10-12 minutes.
  • Grind hazelnuts into a fine paste.
  • Melt chocolate chips in a double boiler
  • Mix powdered sugar, cocoa powder, and salt.
  • Combine hazelnut paste, melted chocolate, and sugar mixture.
  • Add butter and mix until smooth.
  • Spread on toast, crackers, or use as a dip.

Gulab Jamun

Ingredients: milk powder, flour, ghee, milk, sugar, rosewater

Instructions:

  • Mix milk powder, flour, and ghee.
  • Add milk and knead.
  • Shape into balls and deep-fry
  • Soak in sugar syrup with rosewater.

Black Forest

Ingredients: - 2 cups flour - 1 cup sugar - 1 cup cocoa powder - 1 cup milk - 2 eggs - 1 cup whipped cream - 1 cup cherry preserves - 1 cup chocolate shavings

Instructions:

  • Make chocolate cake.
  • Whip cream.
  • Spread cherry preserves on cake.
  • Top with whipped cream and chocolate shavings.
  • Repeat layers.
  • Serve and enjoy!

Ras Malai

Ingredients:paneer, milk, sugar, cardamom, saffron

Instructions:

  • Soak paneer in milk and sugar syrup.
  • Add cardamom and saffron.
  • Chill and serve

Gajar Halwa

Ingredients: - 1 kg grated carrots - 1 cup milk - 1/2 cup sugar - 1/4 cup ghee - 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder - 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg powder - Chopped nuts (optional)

Instructions:

  • Cook grated carrots in milk until tender.
  • Add sugar, ghee, cardamom, and nutmeg.
  • Cook until mixture thickens.
  • Garnish with chopped nuts (if using).
  • Serve warm or chilled
