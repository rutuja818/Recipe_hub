Classic Italian Recipes

Bruschetta

Grilled bread rubbed with garlic and topped with diced tomatoes, basil, and mozzarella cheese.

Instructions:

  • Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  • Slice the bread into 1-inch thick slices.
  • Rub the garlic cloves over the bread slices.
  • Top each bread slice with diced tomatoes, basil, and mozzarella cheese.
  • Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze.
  • Serve immediately.

Spaghetti Carbonara

Tender spaghetti tossed with bacon, eggs, parmesan cheese, and black pepper.

Instructions:

  • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  • Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  • In a large skillet, cook the bacon over medium heat until crispy.
  • In a medium bowl, whisk together the eggs, parmesan cheese, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  • Add the cooked spaghetti to the bowl and toss with the egg mixture.
  • Add the cooked bacon to the bowl and toss everything together.
  • Serve immediately.

Pizza Margherita

Classic Neapolitan-style pizza topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil leaves.

Instructions:

  • Preheat oven to 500°F (260°C) with a pizza stone inside.
  • Roll out the pizza dough to a thickness of 1/4 inch.
  • Transfer the dough to the preheated pizza stone.
  • Spread the tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  • Top the sauce with mozzarella cheese and fresh basil leaves.
  • Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  • Serve immediately.

Chicken Parmesan

Breaded and fried chicken breasts topped with marinara sauce and melted mozzarella cheese.

Instructions:

  • Pound the chicken breasts to a thickness of 1/4 inch.
  • Dip the chicken breasts in the beaten eggs and then coat in the breadcrumbs.
  • Heat about 1/2 inch of olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  • Fry the chicken breasts until golden brown and crispy, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  • Transfer the fried chicken breasts to a baking dish.
  • Spoon marinara sauce over the top of each chicken breast.
  • Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the top of each chicken breast.
  • Bake in a preheated oven at 400°F (200°C) for about 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  • Serve hot and enjoy!

Pasta Con Pomodoro E Basilico

Ingredients: - 12 oz (340g) spaghetti - 2 cups (475ml) fresh tomatoes, diced - 1/4 cup (60g) extra-virgin olive oil - 4 cloves garlic, minced - 1/4 cup (15g) fresh basil leaves, chopped - Salt and pepper to taste - Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  • Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  • in a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  • Add the diced tomatoes to the skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes until they start to release their juices.
  • Stir in the chopped basil leaves and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining the spaghetti.
  • Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with the tomato sauce. Toss everything together, adding some reserved pasta water if the sauce seems too thick.
  • Serve immediately, topped with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
